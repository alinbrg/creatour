About us

CreaTour - Pursuit of an Aspiration to Shape the Meaning of Our Own World and The World We Live In CreaTour is a touristic project following forceful currents of modern times that have gone way out of traditional understanding of tourist travel.

Feeling has become much more widespread that life experience harbours much deeper richness in its meaning rather than in its simple sensory stimulus, superficial recreation, mere excitement, entertainment and fun. At Least for ‘people of certain nature’ there cannot be a true sense of completion or fulfillment without connecting with deeper life-meanings and human values. This need creates powerful human hunger driving the critical evolutionary quest. CreaTour has been borne out of the quest for deep life meanings and values that move us as humans, individuals and societies and around which the human world revolves.

Philosophers and thinkers all around the world, through their quest for deeper meanings and values, have made “existential contribution towards human consciousness” and formation of movements from enlightenment and awakening (Budha), self-realisation by seeking highest meanings and knowledge (Hindu), self-examination and study of outside world (Confucius), exploration of the path to the harmony and balance (Tao), primacy of good thoughts, good words and good deeds (Zoroaster) to ethics and humanism (Christianity and western philosophy) and to current social and political theories that have powerfully influenced flow of history and current social and political order and affected the lives of each of us in profound ways. This quest is a human energy that has a power of such colossal change.

CreaTour is an initiative to support and unite like-minded people in pursuit of aspiration to shape the meaning of our own world and the world we live in. Means of achieving that is traveling through, discovering and experiencing the deepest life-meanings by which we are wired as humans. Goal is to put together the combination of worldwide traveling, social and historic touring and intellectual journeying as means of this empowering discovery.

CreaTour is devotion to the human desire of gaining new life meanings and increasing horizon of consciousness. And because Consciousness has a transformative influence on the course of life, this is designed to be a transformative experience

Rather than being travel experience in its traditionalised sense, CreaTour is designed to be a value experience generating fresh and authentic insights for deep reflection. Being busy pursuing social and individual roles and goals, humans have been suggested or imposed by their living environments into current ways and standards of consciousness. And, as a result, life often feels like prearranged route supposed to be followed rather than the opportunity given to us to connect with our own values that lead us to the pursuit of the life truly meaningful to us.

There is a captivating sentiment felt and expressed since ancient times, that “Unexamined life is not worth living” (Socrates) and the essence of allowing time and space for discovery and reflections is proclaimed by every major philosophy and religion worldwide. Indeed, it makes so much sense to take a break of our lives for the very purpose of searching, clarifying and connecting with life values. And what could be a better place, for the start, than changing the environment for taking a fresh view!

We would not be the first ones to argue that the best way of predicting the future is inventing one – creating it; Best way of defining ourselves is creating who we want to be rather than succumbing to the current flow of life and given “reality” of who we are. The whole life in this sense can be an adventurous Journey and Tour of Creation – new-meaning creation of the Self and the World. Call of this design is to join the march for this high quest - human tour of creation - CreaTour.
